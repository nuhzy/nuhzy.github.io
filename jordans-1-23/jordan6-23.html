Jordan 6's

The Air Jordan 6, designed by Tinker Hatfield and released in 1991, is a classic basketball sneaker known for its sleek silhouette and innovative features. It was Michael Jordan's first championship sneaker, capturing the spirit of his rise to stardom. The shoe boasts a distinctive rubber tongue, unique heel tab, and visible Air cushioning, providing both style and comfort on the court. Its bold colorways and iconic design have made the Air Jordan 6 a favorite among sneaker enthusiasts and collectors, solidifying its status as a timeless classic in the sneaker world.

Jordan Infrared 6's Description

    The Air Jordan 6 "Infrared" is a legendary sneaker that debuted in 1991, marking a pivotal moment in both basketball and sneaker culture. Designed by Tinker Hatfield, this model features a sleek black nubuck upper, accentuated by vibrant Infrared details on the midsole, lace lock, and heel tab. The visible Air cushioning in the heel provides comfort and support, making it a favorite both on and off the court. This colorway is particularly significant as it was worn by Michael Jordan during his first NBA championship season, symbolizing his rise to greatness.
