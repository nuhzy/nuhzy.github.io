Jordan 13's

The Air Jordan 13, released in 1997, is a classic basketball sneaker designed by Tinker Hatfield. It features a sleek silhouette inspired by a black panther, symbolizing Michael Jordan's agility and prowess on the court. The shoe is known for its distinctive holographic "eye" on the heel, premium materials, and responsive cushioning. With its iconic design and rich colorways, the Jordan 13 remains a favorite among sneakerheads and basketball enthusiasts alike.
