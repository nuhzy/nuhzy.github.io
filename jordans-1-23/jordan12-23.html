Jordan 12's

The Air Jordan 12, released in 1996 and designed by Tinker Hatfield, is a standout sneaker known for its unique design and performance features. Drawing inspiration from the Japanese rising sun flag, the shoe features a sleek, leather upper with distinctive stitched lines that create a bold, elegant look. The Air Jordan 12 is celebrated for its comfort and support, featuring a full-length Air unit in the midsole and a durable rubber outsole for traction. Michael Jordan famously wore the 12 during the 1997 NBA season, leading the Chicago Bulls to a championship. Available in various iconic colorways, including the "Taxi" and "Flu Game," the Air Jordan 12 remains a beloved choice for both basketball players and sneaker enthusiasts, symbolizing a perfect blend of style and functionality.

Jordan Taxi 12's Description

    The Air Jordan 12 "Taxi," first released in 1996, is a classic sneaker that showcases a blend of elegance and performance. Designed by Tinker Hatfield, this model features a premium white leather upper, accented by black detailing on the collar and midsole, creating a striking contrast. The shoe is distinguished by its unique stitched lines, which are inspired by the Japanese rising sun flag, giving it a bold and sophisticated appearance. Notable features include metallic gold eyelets and a vibrant Varsity Red Jumpman logo, adding a touch of luxury. Renowned for its comfort, the Air Jordan 12 incorporates a full-length Air unit in the midsole, providing exceptional cushioning and support for athletes. Michael Jordan famously wore the "Taxi" during the 1997 NBA season, further solidifying its iconic status. The Air Jordan 12 "Taxi" remains a highly sought-after model among sneaker enthusiasts, appreciated for its timeless design and deep connection to basketball history.
