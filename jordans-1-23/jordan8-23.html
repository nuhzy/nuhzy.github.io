Jordan 8's

The Air Jordan 8, released in 1993 and designed by Tinker Hatfield, is a distinctive basketball sneaker celebrated for its unique style and performance features. Known for its bold design, the shoe features a combination of premium leather and nubuck, along with striking color blocking and innovative cross-straps for added support and lockdown. The Air Jordan 8 is notable for its plush cushioning and the iconic “23” and Jumpman logos. It was famously worn by Michael Jordan during his historic 1993 NBA Championship season, further solidifying its place in sneaker history.

Jordan Bugs Bunny 8's Description

Designed by Tinker Hatfield, this model features a sleek white leather upper, complemented by black and grey accents, along with vibrant University Red highlights. Worn by Michael Jordan during his historic 1993 season, the Air Jordan 8 "Bugs Bunny" not only boasts a rich legacy but also appeals to sneaker enthusiasts and fans of the beloved Looney Tunes character, solidifying its status as a timeless classic in sneaker history.
