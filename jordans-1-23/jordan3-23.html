Jordan 3's

The Air Jordan 3, released in 1988, is a groundbreaking sneaker designed by Tinker Hatfield. It introduced the iconic Jumpman logo and was the first Jordan to feature visible Air cushioning in the sole, enhancing both comfort and performance. The shoe is recognized for its distinctive elephant print accents, sleek silhouette, and mid-cut design, making it a favorite on and off the court. Available in various colorways, including the legendary "White/Cement," the Jordan 3 is celebrated for its role in Michael Jordan’s rise to stardom and has solidified its status as a classic in sneaker culture.

Jordan Cement 3's Description

    The Air Jordan 3 "White/Cement" is a legendary sneaker that debuted in 1988 and is celebrated for its iconic design. Crafted with a predominantly white leather upper, it features distinctive elephant print accents on the toe and heel, adding a bold touch. The shoe incorporates a visible Air unit in the sole for enhanced cushioning and comfort. Black detailing on the eyelets and Swoosh, along with Fire Red accents on the Jumpman logo and outsole, complete the look. Renowned for its association with Michael Jordan’s historic moments on the court, the "White/Cement" 3s have become a timeless classic in both basketball and sneaker culture.
