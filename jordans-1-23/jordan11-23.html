Jordan 11's

The Air Jordan 11, released in 1995 and designed by Tinker Hatfield, is one of the most iconic sneakers in the Jordan lineup. Known for its sleek silhouette and innovative materials, the Jordan 11 features a combination of patent leather and mesh, offering both style and performance.The shoe is particularly famous for its association with Michael Jordan during his comeback season, where he led the Chicago Bulls to an NBA Championship. Its cushioned midsole provides exceptional comfort, while the translucent outsole adds a modern touch. Available in various colorways, including the legendary "Concord" and "Bred," the Air Jordan 11 is celebrated for its elegance and athletic heritage, making it a must-have for sneaker enthusiasts and basketball fans alike.

Jordan Concord 11's Description

    The Air Jordan 11 "Concord," released originally in 1995 and retroed several times since, is one of the most iconic sneakers in the Jordan lineup. Designed by Tinker Hatfield, this model features a striking combination of premium white leather and a glossy black patent leather mudguard, giving it a sleek and sophisticated appearance. The "Concord" colorway is distinguished by its University Blue accents, notably seen in the Jumpman logo on the heel and the "23" stitched on the back. The shoe also includes a translucent outsole, which not only enhances its aesthetic appeal but also provides excellent traction. This sneaker gained immense popularity when worn by Michael Jordan during the 1995 NBA Playoffs, particularly as he led the Chicago Bulls to a championship. The Air Jordan 11 "Concord" is celebrated for its elegance, innovative design, and deep connection to basketball history, making it a highly sought-after model among sneaker enthusiasts and collectors alike.
