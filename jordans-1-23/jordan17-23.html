Jordan 17's

The Air Jordan 17, released in 2002, is known for its sleek, sophisticated design that reflects a blend of performance and luxury. Inspired by jazz music and Michael Jordan's refined style, the shoe features a premium leather upper with distinctive stitching and a unique lacing system. The standout element is the removable shroud, which adds a stylish twist and allows for a customizable look. Equipped with responsive Zoom Air cushioning and a durable outsole for traction, the Jordan 17 is celebrated for its comfort and performance on the court, making it a favorite among sneaker enthusiasts and collectors.
